Mar del Plata "The happy city"

The City of Mar del Plata is known as "the Happy City"
Mar del Plata is a city in Buenos Aires Province, Argentina located on the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. It is the head of General Pueyrredón Partido.
Mar del Plata is the second largest city in Buenos Aires Province.
The name "Mar del Plata" has the meaning of "sea of the Plate region" or "adjoining sea to the (River) Plate region"
Mar del Plata is one of the major fishing ports and the biggest seaside beach resort in Argentina.
